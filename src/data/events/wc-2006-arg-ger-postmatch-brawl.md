summary_en: "After the penalty shootout of the 2006 World Cup quarter-final between Argentina and Germany, a brawl reportedly erupted between players and staff from both teams. Unused Argentine substitute Leandro Cufré was sent off for kicking Germany's Per Mertesacker, while Maxi Rodríguez was reported to have hit Bastian Schweinsteiger from behind. Germany's Torsten Frings was also reported to have punched Julio Cruz during the altercation. FIFA subsequently investigated video evidence and issued disciplinary suspensions. Host nation Germany won the shootout 4-2."
