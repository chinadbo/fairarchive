summary_en: "The 2022 World Cup CONMEBOL qualifier between Brazil and Argentina was suspended around five minutes in after officials from Brazil's health regulator (ANVISA) entered the pitch, alleging that four England-based Argentine players (Buendía, Martínez, Lo Celso, Romero) had breached COVID-19 quarantine rules; the Argentina team then left the field. The incident sparked a dispute over cross-border health jurisdiction and match compliance. According to media reports citing a subsequent FIFA ruling, the match was ordered to be replayed, the four players were each banned for two matches, and both the Argentine and Brazilian federations were fined; the replay was later cancelled in August 2022 after both teams had already qualified."
