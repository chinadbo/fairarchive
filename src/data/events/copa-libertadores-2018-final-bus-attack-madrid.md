summary_zh: "据报道，在2018年11月24日解放者杯决赛次回合原定开赛前夕，博卡青年队大巴在前往河床主场纪念碑球场的途中遭人群投掷物袭击，车窗破碎致胡椒喷雾进入车厢并造成多名球员受伤。比赛多次延期后，CONMEBOL决定将其移至西班牙马德里伯纳乌球场于12月9日进行，成为解放者杯决赛首次在美洲以外举行。据报道CONMEBOL对河床处以罚款及部分主场空场处罚，河床最终以总比分5-3夺冠。"
summary_en: "Reportedly, ahead of the scheduled second leg of the 2018 Copa Libertadores final on 24 November 2018, the Boca Juniors team bus was attacked by projectiles from a crowd as it travelled to River Plate's Estadio Monumental; broken windows allowed pepper spray into the cabin and injured several players. After repeated postponements, CONMEBOL relocated the match to the Santiago Bernabéu in Madrid on 9 December, the first time a Copa Libertadores final was held outside the Americas. CONMEBOL was reported to have fined River Plate and ordered closed-door home matches; River won 5-3 on aggregate."
