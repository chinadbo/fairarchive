summary_en: "On 10 July 2021, Argentina beat Brazil 1-0 in the Copa América final at Rio's Maracanã, Di María scoring in the 22nd minute to end a 28-year senior title drought and give Messi his first senior international trophy. The match was reportedly physical, with 49 fouls and 9 yellow cards, and a Brazil equaliser by Richarlison was ruled out for offside. Afterwards Brazil coach Tite reportedly called the final \"stop-start\" and \"anti-football\" and again criticised the tournament organisation; Argentina did not publicly respond to the characterisation."
