summary_en: "FIFA reportedly handed Argentina goalkeeper Emiliano Martínez a two-match ban on 28 September 2024 for violating principles of fairplay in two separate incidents. The first allegedly involved him holding a replica Copa América trophy to his groin after Argentina's World Cup qualifier win over Chile on 5 September; the second allegedly involved him striking a television camera after Argentina's 2-1 loss to Colombia on 10 September. The ban ruled him out of Argentina's October 2024 qualifiers against Venezuela and Bolivia."
