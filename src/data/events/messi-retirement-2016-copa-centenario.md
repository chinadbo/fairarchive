summary_zh: "据报道，2016年6月26日百年美洲杯决赛在纽约大都会人寿球场举行，智利经0-0平局后点球4-2击败阿根廷；比赛中阿根廷的罗霍与智利的马塞洛·迪亚兹均在上半场被红牌罚下，梅西在点球大战中射失首球。赛后梅西据报向记者表示“我不打算再为国家队效力了”，并称“四次决赛我都未能夺冠”。这是阿根廷连续第三次大赛决赛失利。同年8月梅西收回退役决定，称“决赛那天我脑子里有太多事”。"
summary_en: "On 26 June 2016, Chile beat Argentina 4-2 on penalties after a 0-0 draw in the Copa América Centenario final at MetLife Stadium, New Jersey; Argentina's Rojo and Chile's Marcelo Díaz were both sent off in the first half, and Messi missed Argentina's opening penalty. After the match Messi reportedly told reporters \"I'm done playing with the national team,\" citing four finals lost. It was Argentina's third consecutive major final defeat. He reversed the decision in August 2016, saying \"there were too many things in my head during the day of the last final.\""
