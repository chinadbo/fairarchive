summary_en: "In the 1998 World Cup round of 16 between Argentina and England, Danish referee Kim Milton Nielsen sent off England's David Beckham in the 47th minute. Argentina's Diego Simeone had fouled Beckham and allegedly rubbed his knuckles against the back of Beckham's head; Beckham, lying face-down, swung his leg at Simeone, who fell, prompting the red card. Simeone reportedly later admitted to simulating the injury from the kick to get Beckham sent off. England lost 4-3 on penalties. Additionally, a late Sol Campbell goal was disallowed for a foul by Alan Shearer on the Argentine goalkeeper, also sparking debate."
