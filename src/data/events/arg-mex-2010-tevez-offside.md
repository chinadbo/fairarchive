summary_en: "In the 2010 World Cup round-of-16 match where Argentina beat Mexico 3–1, Carlos Tevez reportedly headed in from a Lionel Messi pass in the 26th minute while clearly offside, with no defender between him and the goal. The goal stood, and the offside replay was shown inside the stadium. The referee was Italy's Roberto Rosetti. Mexico disputed the decision, though coach Javier Aguirre said he would not blame the referee for the defeat. The call was not officially overturned."
