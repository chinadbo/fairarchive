summary_zh: "据报道，1994年世界杯期间，马拉多纳在对阵尼日利亚的小组赛（6月25日）后接受药检，结果被检出含有麻黄碱（ephedrine），一种被列为违禁的减重刺激性药物。据《纽约时报》报道，在第二次检测确认阳性后，FIFA将马拉多纳逐出该届赛事。马拉多纳辩称阳性结果源于其体能教练误购了美国版能量饮料Rip Fuel（含有麻黄碱成分），他还声称此前曾与FIFA达成允许其使用该药物减重的协议，但FIFA据称未予履行。阿根廷随后在八分之一决赛被罗马尼亚淘汰。"
summary_en: "During the 1994 World Cup, Maradona was reportedly subjected to an anti-doping test after Argentina's group match against Nigeria (June 25), which uncovered ephedrine, a banned stimulant. According to The New York Times, after a second confirmatory test, FIFA expelled Maradona from the tournament. Maradona claimed the positive result stemmed from his personal trainer unwittingly purchasing the US version of an energy drink called Rip Fuel, which contained ephedrine. He also alleged a prior agreement with FIFA to use the substance for weight loss, which the organization reportedly reneged on. Argentina was subsequently eliminated by Romania in the round of 16."
